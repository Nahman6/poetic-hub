I am a servant of the Lord.
The strength she gives me is the only reason I exist.
I often write poetry inspired by the words of my beloved Guru and below is one of them.



She is everything. There are simply no words to describe Her Grace.






Her Grace

Caresses your hair,
Tucks it back.
Holds you near,
Kisses your crown.

Shows the truth,
Shadows Ruth.
Braces you,
Completes you.

Her presence, utter bliss,
Her words, all of wisdom.
Her smile, brings peace,
Her love, Elation.
